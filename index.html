About Me

My passion is fitness, more specifically running. I believe that running has the ability to change one's life. That's the reason I started my current side hustle called Summersault Running Costumes. Its purpose is to inspire fitness by making running fun with running costumes. We supply running costumes which are suitable for running in our humid Malaysian climate. We believe that running costumes have the ability to create camaradarie amongst beginner runners, making them enjoy running more and thus, encouraging runners to continue running until the habit is formed.
